Teddy Chen, the founder of Teddysoft, is an agile consultant and trainer. He is the author of "Laughing at Software Engineering: The Counterattack of Agile Development Method" and "Laughing at Software Engineering: The Counterattack of Exception Handling Design." Teddy is also the moderator of the "Funny Talk about Software Engineering" blog and serves as an adjunct assistant professor at the Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering at Taipei University of Technology. He teaches two courses on Agile and Lean Software Development and Software Architecture.
